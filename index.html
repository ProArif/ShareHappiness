About Us

We are a bunch of tech enthusiast peoples trying to help needy peoples using our platform ShareHappiness, a Mobile app where People can share their extra foods as well as cloths through this app and also can be a volunteer to distribute those extra foods and cloths to poor peoples. There are too many peoples living in the streets they don’t get proper foods and cloths but we are wasting too much foods everyday. Why not help others instead of wasting valuable food?
